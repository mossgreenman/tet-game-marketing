About this game
Experience the Joy of Vietnamese TET with our game!
Step into the wonderful world of TET, the Vietnamese Lunar New Year, with our colorful game! Experience like a native through engaging activities and delightful mini-games that showcase the rich traditions and delicious cuisine of Vietnam.
Discover traditional dishes like Banh Chung, a savory sticky rice cake wrapped in banana leaves, and Gac sticky rice, a vibrant red sticky rice infused with Gac fruit (Momordica cochinchinensis). TET Jam, a sweet assortment of preserved fruits, and Bamboo Soup, a warm and comforting dish perfect for family gatherings.
Explore and learn about the significance of unique TET fruits and objects, such as the fragrant Buddha's Hand fruit and the auspicious Green Bananas. Each item in the game is meticulously designed to reflect the authenticity and cultural importance of Vietnamese Lunar New Year.
Perfect for all ages, our game not only entertains but also educates players about the customs and traditions that make TET a memorable experience. Whether you're familiar with TET or discovering it for the first time, our game offers a captivating experience that brings the spirit of Vietnam Lunar New Year to you.
Download now and join with us! Embrace the traditions, taste the flavors, and share the joy of TET with family and friends through our delightful game.
Happy New Year 2025!!
